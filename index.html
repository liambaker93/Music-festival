Introducing Through The Trees

Through The Trees has been growing each year since the first year as an antidote to big corporate festivals.

We've been aiming to put the focus back on to artists and not take away any of your concentration from them. Sporting one stage showcasing the greatest upcoming talent, Through The Trees is proud to bring you along for the ride.

Take a look at the artists on offer, some of the unique, local food we have for you to enjoy, andbook your weekend ticket now.
